Team

Prabhas Pokharel is a Social Entrepreur with a background in Design Thinking and Computer Science. He has worked around the world solving social issues in the developing world with design and technology. He feels proudest of his work that has resulted in increased human capability, whether it is for young people in Kosovo to engage in social change, kids with cleft palate in Ecuador to speak better, or Indian nurse midwives to serve their rural clients better. Prabhas has a Masters in Mechanical Engineering from the Stanford Design Program, and an undergraduate degree in Computer Science from Harvard College.




Maya Sussman is passionate about designing products and programs to make education more equitable, engaging, and accessible for learners of all ages and backgrounds. She previously helped students and teachers get easy access to online learning at Clever, and started a summer camp for immigrant youth in the Boston area. Maya has a Masters in Education from Stanford's Learning, Design, and Technology program, and a BA in International Literary and Visual Studies from Tufts University.




Teagan Daly is a Product Designer with roots in design thinking, visual design, and engineering. After graduating from Dartmouth, Teagan co-founded a digital innovation lab on campus (the DALI Lab) where she led design for 25 concurrent student teams. This year, Teagan was the recipient of the Bill Moggridge award for excellence in interaction design. David Kelley, who co-founded IDEO with Bill Moggridge, selected Teagan for the first time this award was given to a Stanford student.
